Pathfinder Race Builder

This tool allows Pathfinder players to quickly assemble custom playable races using Paizo's optional race builder rules. Always consult with your GM before using races generated by this tool in actual play -- the expected power level of your race and how wide (or narrow) you want to leave its gameplay options should be mutually determined for the benefit of the game.

Examples of SRD races reconstructed with the Race Builder include:

  • 6 RP - Ifrit
  • 8 RP - Orc
  • 10 RP - Goblin
  • 13 RP - Tiefling
  • 15 RP - Aasimar
